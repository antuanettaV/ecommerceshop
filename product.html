Lancome La Vie Est Belle

La Vie Est Belle, French for "life is beautiful," is a statement on the world around us, infused with meaning, with both a conscience and a soul. La Vie Est Belle is for the woman who lives in the present, who is filled with honesty, truth and gratitude, and who is free of social constraint, to emblazon her own unique path. La Vie Est Belle represents a choice - the choice to create your own path to happiness. La Vie Est Belle is the fragrance of joyful femininity that makes life more beautiful. Made with the most precious natural ingredients, La Vie Est Belle entwines the elegance of iris with the strength of patchouli and the sweetness of praline. Floral notes of Sambac, Jasmine, and Tunisian Orange Blossom are added in the form of sublime absolutes.
